# 🧪 Hyderabad Specialty Biotech & Chemical Manufacturers — Target Company Analysis

## 📌 Project Summary
This project focuses on identifying and evaluating **high-quality manufacturing companies** in Hyderabad within the **specialty biotech and specialty chemicals/API segments**.

The objective was to build a **curated list of 25 companies** that demonstrate:
- Strong manufacturing capabilities  
- Technical product differentiation  
- Credible leadership  
- Clear growth signals  

The analysis is based on a structured evaluation framework (“Federer Profile”) applied consistently across all companies.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify **true manufacturers** (not service companies)
- Focus on **niche, technical, and export-oriented products**
- Apply a **data-driven scoring model**
- Build a **high-confidence target company list**

---

## 🏭 Scope Definition
- **Location:** Hyderabad, India  
- **Segments Covered:**
  - Specialty Biotech (vaccines, biologics)
  - Specialty Chemicals / APIs  
- **Explicit Exclusions:**
  - CRO / CDMO / testing services  
  - Pure distributors or traders  
  - Companies without manufacturing proof  

---

## ⚙️ Methodology Overview

### **1. Company Sourcing**
Initial company list was created using:
- Search queries (biotech/API manufacturers)
- Industry directories and platforms  
- Company websites  
- LinkedIn (for leadership and hiring signals)

---

### **2. Multi-Layer Filtering**
Companies were filtered in stages:
- Removal of service-based firms  
- Verification of manufacturing facilities  
- Elimination of non-relevant or generic businesses  

---

### **3. Evaluation Framework (Federer Criteria)**

Each company was assessed across 6 dimensions:

| Criterion | Description |
|----------|------------|
| **C1** | Manufacturing capability (own plants, production) |
| **C2** | India-based operations |
| **C3** | Product differentiation (APIs, biologics, approvals) |
| **C4** | Technical leadership (founder/MD background) |
| **C5** | Sector tailwinds (growth potential) |
| **C6** | Growth signals (expansion, hiring, approvals) |

---

### **4. Scoring System**

Each criterion was graded as:
- **Strong**
- **Moderate**
- **Weak**

Final score calculated out of **100**

| Score Range | Category | Interpretation |
|------------|--------|----------------|
| 80–100 | A — Strong Federer | High-priority target |
| 60–79 | B — Probable Federer | Good fit with minor gaps |
| <60 | Not Selected | Does not meet requirements |

---

## 📊 Dataset Structure (Excel File)

The dataset is designed to reflect **real-world company evaluation**, not just basic listing.

Each company includes:

- **Basic Information**
  - Company Name  
  - Website  
  - Segment  
  - Products  

- **Business Insights**
  - Revenue Band (estimated)  
  - Decision Maker  

- **Evaluation Layer**
  - C1–C6 Scores  
  - Evidence for each criterion  

- **Final Output**
  - Federer Score  
  - Score Band  
  - Verdict  
  - Reasoning  

- **Outreach Layer**
  - Personalization Hook (for targeted communication)

👉 This structure ensures the dataset is **usable for real business decisions**, not just academic work.
